Stingless bee (locally called dammer bees in many parts of India) are a group of small, non-stinging bees that produce medicinal honey. These bees are commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

They build their nests in:

Tree cavities
Wall cracks
Wooden boxes (for managed beekeeping)

## 🌟 Why Dammer Bees are Important

1. Safe Beekeeping

Dammer bees do not sting, making them ideal for beginners, women, and community-based projects.

2. High-Value Medicinal Honey

Their honey is known for its medicinal properties and is often sold at a higher price than regular honey.

3. Excellent Pollinators

They play a crucial role in pollinating crops, improving agricultural productivity.

4. Low Maintenance

They require less space and investment compared to species like Apis mellifera.

## Unique Honey Characteristics

Dammer bee honey is different from regular honey:

More liquid and slightly sour in taste

Rich in antioxidants and antibacterial properties

Used in traditional medicine for immunity and healing
